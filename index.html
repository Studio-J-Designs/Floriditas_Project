About



Situated in the heart of Cuba Street, Floriditas is constantly striving for that magical combination of delicious tasty food, beautifully crafted wines and slick service.

New Zealand produce influenced by the cuisines and techniques of the Mediterranean. Clean flavours that let the ingredients speak for themselves.

Open all day we offer simple, delicious, tasty seasonal food to from local and national producers.

We also have wines from all New Zealand’s regions and taste of what the rest of the world has to offer.
